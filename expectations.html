About House Expectations

Short and sweet things we expect from all housemates.

FOR ALL HOUSEMATES

Clean up after yourself.

Every time you've finished using the kitchen and dining room area:

  • Wash, dry (with rag or paper towel), and put away your dishes.
  • Wipe down counters and stove (including the circles).
  • Wipe down the table area where you ate.
  • Put away anything you used.

A good metric: If you wouldn't run your hands over the area, you should clean it.

Every time you've finished using the living room area:

  • Put away anything you used.

Buy communal supplies if shopping.

Check group chat if anyone's noticed any supplies that are needed.

Do not assume someone else will do it. Reply to chat saying you'll buy them.

Send receipt to Erick. He will charge housemates at the end of the month and pay back those who bought supplies.

FOR HOUSE MANAGER

Pay rent and other bills in a timely manner.

Do not wait until last minute. Homelessness is not cool.

Other bills: communal supplies, landscaping, housekeeping.

Escalate complaints to leasing office.

Do not postpone. Continue to complain until action is taken or answer is given.

List of Pending Items: Leasing Office Dealings

Manage housemate move-outs.

Is someone moving out? When? Is room damaged?

Find new housemates who meet agreed criteria.

CLEAN and ORGANIZED and will vibe well with the housemates. Maintain M/F ratio.

Call house meetings when appropriate.

Only include those needed. Only call meetings when required. Don't waste time.

Ensure adherence to agreed-upon rules.

Send reminders on group chat of what we agreed upon. Make sure others follow agreed-upon rules.
